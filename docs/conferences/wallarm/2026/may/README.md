### About

This upcoming Wallarm webinar focuses on the security blind spots traditional WAFs have when AI-connected systems and AI-generated traffic are in production. The session centers on why signature-based detection breaks down in AI-first environments and what behavioral detection and positive security models look like in practice.

### What attendees will walk away with

- A clearer picture of where a WAF is blind to AI system traffic
- Better context for evaluating behavioral detection and ML-driven approaches
- A framework for making the case internally to move beyond rules-based protection
- Concrete next steps to act on immediately
