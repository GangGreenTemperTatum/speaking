# [National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine](https://www.nationalacademies.org/) - Securing AI Systems
## Securing AI Systems: New Challenges and Research Priorities

- **Event:** Securing AI Systems: New Challenges and Research Priorities
- **Date:** April 20-21, 2026
- **Location:** National Academy of Sciences Building, 2101 Constitution Ave NW, Washington DC 20418, USA
- **Organization:** National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine (at NSF request)
- **Format:** Convening / Workshop

### About

This convening aims to identify research priorities for securing machine learning and AI-enabled systems. It will examine measurement frameworks for AI security, assess how traditional cybersecurity approaches adapt to AI contexts, and explore emerging risks in applications like scientific research and drug discovery.

### Session 3: Threat Modeling for AI Security (Day 1 — Monday, April 20, 2026)

**1:00 - 2:40 PM**

This session will examine adversaries, emerging attack surfaces, failure modes, and trust boundaries. It will consider how threat modeling must evolve to account for advancing AI capabilities, with examples from key application domains.
